How Quarantine Works

🔒 Quarantined → 👁 Analyst Reviews → ✓ True Phishing: keep quarantined + resolve alert
or → False Positive: mark safe, release to user, resolve alert

Why not auto-delete? ML models aren't perfect. A false positive (legitimate email wrongly flagged) could make someone miss an important message. Human review before any action is the professional standard in enterprise security.
